# Free and Low-Cost Activities for Peer Support Programs in Portland Metro Area

This comprehensive guide provides **verified street addresses, 2026 schedules, and pricing** for activities appropriate for low-income adults and vulnerable populations in Multnomah and Clackamas Counties. All activities are free or under $10, exclude alcohol-focused events, and emphasize social connection.

---

## U-Pick Farms: Affordable Harvesting Experiences

The Portland metro area offers exceptional u-pick opportunities, with several farms accepting **SNAP/EBT, WIC vouchers, and Senior Farm Direct Nutrition Program vouchers**—critical for low-income participants.

### Farms Accepting Assistance Programs

**South Barlow Berries** stands out as the most accessible option for peer support programs, accepting Oregon Trail Card (EBT/SNAP), Senior Farm Direct Vouchers, and WIC Vouchers.
- **Address:** 29190 S Barlow Road, Canby, OR 97013
- **Phone:** 503-266-3193
- **2026 Season:** June–August
- **Pricing:** U-pick berries typically **$3–6/lb**
- **Hours:** Mon–Fri 9am–6pm, Sat 9am–5pm
- **Crops:** Strawberries, raspberries, marionberries, boysenberries, thornless blackberries

**Columbia Farms U-Pick** accepts Farm Direct Vouchers and offers a quiet, non-festival atmosphere ideal for those who prefer calmer environments.
- **Address:** 21024 NW Gillihan Road, Portland, OR 97231 (Sauvie Island)
- **Phone:** 503-621-3909
- **2026 Season:** June–August
- **Hours:** 9am–5pm Tuesday–Sunday
- **Note:** Free pint boxes provided; 15+ berry varieties
