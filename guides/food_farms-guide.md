## Three Programs That Change Everything

1. **Portland Parks Access Discount** — Up to 90% off all parks programs. No proof of income required. [portland.gov/parks/discount](https://portland.gov/parks/discount)
2. **Arts for All** — $5 tickets at 100+ cultural venues with Oregon Trail Card. [racc.org/artsforall](https://racc.org/artsforall)
3. **TriMet Low-Income Fare** — $28/month unlimited transit. [trimet.org/fares/reduced](https://trimet.org/fares/reduced)

## Tips for Those with Social Anxiety

Many resources in this guide are marked as **anxiety-friendly**. Look for these qualities:

- **Drop-in format** — arrive and leave when you want, no commitment
- **Solo activities** — parks, trails, libraries, museums
- **Quiet times** — early mornings, weekday afternoons
- **Low-pressure groups** — craft nights, walking groups where conversation is optional
- **Online options** — Zoom support groups let you participate with camera off
