Free & Low-Cost Activities for Peer Support Programs

What is this? A carefully maintained list of free and low-cost activities, services, and resources for adults in the Portland metro area designed especially for people on limited incomes and those connected to peer support and mental health programs.

What Makes It Useful

Every Resource Includes

  • Address and phone number
  • Cost and available discounts
  • Who qualifies (eligibility)
  • First-visit tips
  • Accessibility information
  • Social comfort level

How People Can Use It

  • Printed guides handouts for those who prefer paper
  • Calendar subscriptions events sync to phone calendars
  • Staff reference quick lookups during sessions

Why This Matters

Finding affordable activities and services is hard. Information online is often outdated, scattered across dozens of websites, or assumes you already know how things work.

This guide puts everything in one place, written in plain language, and keeps it current so peer specialists can confidently share accurate information with the people they support.
