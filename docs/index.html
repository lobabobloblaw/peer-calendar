About This Calendar

A living guide to free and low-cost resources for adults in the Portland tri-county area (Multnomah, Clackamas, and Washington counties).

This calendar is designed with peer support programs and mental health communities in mind—but it's for anyone looking to stay active, connected, and engaged on a limited budget.

What's included

  • Support groups and peer-led programs
  • Free fitness classes and wellness activities
  • Arts, culture, and library events
  • Community meals and food resources
  • Discount programs (transit, utilities, recreation)
  • Social activities and volunteer opportunities

How it works

Subscribe to the calendar using your preferred platform (Apple, Google, or Outlook) and events will sync automatically. We verify each resource and update the calendar regularly.
